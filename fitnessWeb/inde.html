Push-Up

Muscle Group: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

Description: The push-up is a classic bodyweight exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps. Begin in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your body in a straight line from head to heels. Lower your chest towards the ground by bending your elbows, then push back up to the starting position.

Variations: There are many variations of push-ups, including incline push-ups, decline push-ups, and diamond push-ups, which target different muscle groups and provide varying levels of difficulty.

Tips: Keep your core engaged and your body in a straight line throughout the movement. Focus on maintaining proper form and controlled movement to maximize the effectiveness of the exercise.
